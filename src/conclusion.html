Conclusion

Sierra Leone is a quickly developing country with a lot of potential. The government is currently stable and they are recovering from the Ebola epidemic. If there is a perfect time to help develop this strong nation, it is now. The new ACE submarine cable is connecting Sierra Leone to the global internet network and reducing the cost of communications dramatically [1].

Now is the time to develop the nation's infrastructure to support this new outside connection and not let it go to waste. Expanding and making the cellular network more robust will connect even more of the population with a reliable communication platform. Introducing cost effective mobile data to cellular subscribers will quickly benefit the nation as the population gets introduced to the world wide web and it's infinite possibilities and opportunities. These opportunities include the ability to allow citizens to participate in public health, safety, and infrastructure.
